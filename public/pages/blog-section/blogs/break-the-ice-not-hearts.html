How to Set the Scene

First things first: setting the mood. Valentine’s Day might call for candles and soft music, but why not mix in a playful vibe too? Here’s how you can create the perfect atmosphere for a Never Have I Ever date night:


• Pick Your Space: Whether you’re at home or away, choose somewhere comfortable. Blankets, cushions, fairy lights – it’s all about making the vibe relaxed and inviting.

Flirty and Fun: Game Night with a Twist

What makes Never Have I Ever such a perfect fit for Valentine’s? It’s playful, revealing, and allows you to connect without forcing anything too heavy. Whether you’re in the honeymoon phase or you’ve seen each other at your absolute worst, the game works for everyone.


Here are a few ways to put your own spin on it:

If you’re on a first date or early in the relationship, stick to lighthearted prompts from the “Simple Admissions” category. Think:


• “Never have I ever had a pet with a ridiculous name.”


• “Never have I ever sung karaoke sober.”


These kinds of questions keep the vibe fun and low-pressure while still giving you insights into your partner’s quirks. The less serious at first, the better. It’s a nice warm up for you and your love.


2. The “Deep Dive”

For couples who’ve been together a bit longer, the “Digging Deeper” pack is where it’s at. This category is perfect for bringing out stories you’ve never shared or things you didn’t know about each other. Imagine discovering something like:
